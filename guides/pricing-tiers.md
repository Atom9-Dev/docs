# Pricing & Tier Model (Current)

## Free
- Roles: `superuser`, `user`
- Security level cap: `basic`
- Core auth methods can be enabled per policy

## Pro
- Roles: `owner`, `admin`, `user`, `guest`
- Security level cap: `strong`
- Better control for app onboarding and policy hardening

## Business
- Custom role model extension path
- Security level cap: `maximum`
- Highest policy strictness and advanced controls

> Note: Public pricing numbers and limits are published on `atom9.com` when commercial packaging is finalized.
